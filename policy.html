Privacy Policy for TASCE Staff Mobile App

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

The TASCE Staff Mobile App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure.

Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, staff ID)
  • Employment information
  • Device information
  • Login credentials

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide salary information
  • Authenticate users
  • Improve app functionality
  • Send notifications
  • Generate reports

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is encrypted during transmission
  • Secure storage using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to authorized personnel

Data Sharing

We share information only with:

  • TASCE administration
  • Authorized government bodies
  • Service providers (with confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data correction
  • Delete your account
  • Opt-out of communications

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact: support@tasce.edu.ng
